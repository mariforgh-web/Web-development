About aliens

Aliens are hypothetical beings from other planets or galaxies. Scientists and enthusiasts have speculated about their existence for centuries. While no definitive proof exists, many UFO sightings and unusual phenomena have fueled theories about intelligent extraterrestrial life. Aliens are often imagined in popular culture with diverse forms and abilities, ranging from humanoid shapes to completely unimaginable creatures.
